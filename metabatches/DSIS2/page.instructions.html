You will be asked to rate the level of impairment of several test videos with respect to the reference.

The Test video always follows the Reference

When you watch the videos, please rate the level of impairments according to the scale:

5 Imperceptible
4 Perceptible, but not annoying
3 Slightly annoying
2 Annoying
1 Very annoying

Before the actual test, you will have the training session. You will watch 3 training pairs of videos to help you understand the rating scale.

Please note that the video will start playing automatically, and will only be played once; you should clic Next only when you are ready to watch the sequence.
